Innovative China Enters into Jinan

Jinan · Zhongguancun Collaborative Innovation Forum

05.07.2019

In order to further implement the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei integration strategy and the Jinan-Laiwu integration strategy, to strengthen the status of the central city of the south wing of the Bohai Sea area in Jinan, to accelerate the development of the economic circle of the provincial capital, to achieve substantial progress and complete the site selection in cooperation between Jinan and Zhongguancun.


On July 25th, 2019 (Thursday), Sheraton Jinan Hotel, the Jinan and Zhongguancun will jointly hold the Jinan · Zhongguancun Collaborative Innovation Forum, focusing on key industries such as pharmaceutical health and advanced manufacturing, bringing together high-end resources, integrating innovation elements, and organizing industry leaders and innovative enterprises. Representatives of scientific research institutes, science, and technology service institutions and financial institutions will gather together and conduct in-depth exchanges to comprehensively promote the synergy, innovation, and cooperation to achieve the win-win situation between Beijing and Jinan.


Agenda:


09：00 －10：00 Results and presentations of the first key enterprises
10：00 －10：10 Leader’s welcome speech of Jinan city and Zhongguancun
10：10 －10：15 Work report of Zhongguancun Xinxigu Company
10：15 －10：20 achievement presentation of Jinan · Zhongguancun Xinxigu innovation center and demonstration base
10：20 －10：25 Launching ceremony of Jinan · Zhongguancun Xinxigu demonstration base
10：25 －10：30 Signing ceremony for the first 20 settled enterprises and institutions
10：30 －10：40 Appointment ceremony of expert advisor
10：40 －11：05 Settled enterprises representative’s speech
11：05 －11：35 Roundtable dialogue
14：30 －16：30 Discussion on intended enterprises

The forum invited representatives from Germany, Israel, South Korea, as well as Beijing, Shanghai, Shenzhen, and other innovative pioneering regions to jointly build an open and cooperative, collaborative development of international innovation platform to create a regional innovation highland. Deutsches Lächeln GmbH will join the forum with the representatives of Bonn-Rhein-Sieg University, Fourier AG, FerroSens, Coskun and other German technology and innovation enterprises. We sincerely invite you to take the time to participate in the event with us, and we hope that you can find new opportunities for innovation and development.
