5G+AI: A New Era of Intelligent Connectivity under The Industrial Internet

Cooperation between China and Germany brings a win-win situation

31.05.2019

The 2019 China New Generation Artificial Intelligence Development Report was officially released on 24 May 2019 during the Pujiang Innovation Forum. The report reviews the overall situation of China’s Artificial Intelligence development over the past year from the perspectives of the global situation, innovation, environment, scientific research breakthroughs, economic development, social application, and reveals new situations and challenges for future development.


At present, the strategic deployment of AI is moving from a few major countries to a new pattern of “global distribution”. China and the United States are the core nodes of the global artificial intelligence cooperation network. However, the ongoing trade war between China and the United States and the sudden sanctions imposed by the United States on Huawei have once again drawn people’s attention to the future development and cooperation trend of 5G and AI.


5G＋AI two “keys”: Dual-core drive brings “connection of everything”


Many insiders see 5G and AI as two keys, which can connect everything by fusion of two technologies. Under the “dual-core drive” affection of 5G and AI, various industries are promoted to start the transformation and upgrading of digitization, networking, and intelligence, the transformation porch is gradually reduced, and all industries begin to integrate. From the "connection of people " to the "connection of everything", we have gradually entered the era of the Industrial Internet.


5G driving force＋AI catalyst: "Multiplication effect" brings disruptive innovation


With the help of the Industrial Internet, some enterprises have also changed from the traditional "hopping with one foot" to "running with two legs" in collaboration with the Internet. Experts believe that the change from "Internet +" to "+ Internet" is not simple addition, but a multiplication. Many traditional industries have gone from "touching the Internet" to "going on the cloud" to actively embrace the Industrial Internet.


5G＋AI two“Bridges”：Mutual strategic brings win-win cooperation


As a manufacturing power, Germany has been a world leader in the extensive application of automation and informationization in the production process in the industrial 3.0 era. With the proposal of the concept of "Industry 4.0", the intelligent production in Germany has gradually become mature, and many application scenarios and solutions that have been implemented have also been demonstrated during Hannover Messe 2019. Influenced by "Industry 4.0", China has successively put forward the policy of "Made in China 2025", the core idea of which is intelligent manufacturing, aiming to promote the deep integration of industrialization and informationization of traditional Chinese industries, and realize the successful transformation of China from a big industrial country to an industrial power. Under the promotion of this policy, China and Germany have carried out all-round deep cooperation in intelligent production and other fields to jointly promote innovative development.


Artificial Intelligence and 5G networks are new highlights of "Made in China 2025" and "Industry 4.0" strategy. The two sides are highly complementary in technology research and development, product supply and market demand, and have unlimited cooperation opportunities. The cooperation and development of these two technologies are like two Bridges, which can not only synergize their strategies, but also promote long-term cooperation between enterprises in manufacturing, science and technology, innovation industries in two countries, and achieve win-win cooperation.


As a bridge of information exchange and communication between China and Europe, Deutsches Laecheln GmbH focuses on industry development and the policy trend, maintain cooperation widely with industrial enterprises, associations and institutions. We have also organized many activities like Sino-German Industry 4.0 Innovation Forum, seminars between Chinese and German enterprises, field trip of intelligent robot factory in Germany, aiming to build bridges and stable cooperation platform between China and Germany and promote the exchange of enterprises of two sides.
