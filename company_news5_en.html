“2019 Nanjing Enterprise Global Network Interactive Talent Job Fair （Germany）”

21.06.2019

On the afternoon of June 22, the inaugural Nanjing Enterprise Global Interactive Network Talent Job Fair is held successfully in Düsseldorf by Deutsches Lächeln GmbH. Open to talents from the United States, Australia, the United Kingdom, France and Germany, this fair is focused on attracting international professionals and entrepreneurs from a cross-section of industries to develop enterprises in Nanjing and to build a bridge between international talent and established enterprises in Nanjing.


More than 50 Nanjing’s leading enterprises and new scientific research institutes will participate in the fair, including JSTI Group Co., Ltd., Unicom Internet of Things Co., Ltd., Jiangsu Jinzhi Education Information Co., Ltd., Five Star Holdings Co., Ltd., Simcere Pharmaceutical Industry and Nanjing Yanchang Reaction Research Institute Co., Ltd. They represent a broad cross-section of industries including: new information technology, artificial intelligent equipment, biomedicine, new energy conservation and environmental protection materials, software and information services, financial and technological services, etc. More than 200 high-level job opportunities will be made available during the fair.

The high-quality opportunities offered by these enterprises provide high-caliber innovative and entrepreneurial talent not only competitive remuneration but excellent career prospects and an enviable quality of life.


Enterprises participating in the job fair all said that the job fair has effectively improved the efficiency, time and economic cost, and is a win-win high-quality platform connecting domestic enterprises and overseas students. The students who participated in this fair also expressed that they had the opportunity to communicate with their favorite enterprises face to face and get to know the most cutting-edge industry information in China. They had big gains and look forward to more job fairs of this type in the future.

Deutsches Lächeln GmbH is an international service enterprise dedicated to facilitating the exchange and cooperation of market information, technology, and economy between China and Europe by providing customized services in precise and well-defined marketing strategy, PR-services and project management and international trade support with our professional business Know-How.


With our top-notch integrated services and specialization in consulting, digital, trade and strategies, Deutsches Lächeln GmbH solves toughest challenges for our clients, provide professional solutions, seize the opportunities and empower the future together.
